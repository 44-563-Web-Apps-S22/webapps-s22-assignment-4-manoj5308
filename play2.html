BEL.

Begin quickly and hasten our pleasure.

PHI.

Offer your epigram to our impatience.

BEL.

And I am dying for them.

TRI.

(_to_PHILAMINTE)

Alas! it is but a new-born child, Madam, but its fate ought truly to touch your heart, for it was in your court-yard that I brought it forth, but a moment since.
